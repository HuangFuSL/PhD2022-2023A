    \paragraph*{2.15}

    $\lim _{n\rightarrow\infty} (EN_x / x) = 1 / \mu = 1 / (EX + EY)$，由于$Y\sim \mathcal U[0, 1]$，有$EY = 1/2$，已知$x$的分布函数$F(x) = 1 - e^{-x}$，有

    \begin{derive}[EX]
        &= \int_{0}^\infty x\dd F(x) \\
        &= \int_{0}^\infty xe^{-x} \dd x \\
        &= 1
    \end{derive}

    因此$\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty} (EN_x / x) = 2 / 3$
